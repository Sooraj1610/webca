Our Specialty Departments

Our hospital offers comprehensive specialty care across various medical disciplines, with expert physicians and state-of-the-art facilities.

Cardiology

Comprehensive heart care including diagnostics, treatment, and rehabilitation for all cardiac conditions.

Services: Angioplasty, ECG, Stress Test, Holter Monitoring

Neurology

Expert care for disorders of the nervous system, including stroke management and epilepsy treatment.

Services: EEG, EMG, Nerve Conduction Studies

Orthopedics

Specialized care for musculoskeletal conditions, joint replacements, and sports injuries.

Services: Joint Replacement, Arthroscopy, Fracture Management

Oncology

Comprehensive cancer care including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgical oncology.

Services: Chemotherapy, Radiation Therapy, Cancer Screening

Pediatrics

Specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents in a child-friendly environment.

Services: Vaccinations, Growth Monitoring, Pediatric ICU

Gastroenterology

Diagnosis and treatment of digestive system disorders including endoscopy and colonoscopy.

Services: Endoscopy, Colonoscopy, Liver Disease Management
