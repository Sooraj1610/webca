What is Telemedicine?

Telemedicine allows patients to consult with healthcare providers remotely using technology. This service is designed to provide convenient access to medical care from the comfort of your home.

How to Access Telemedicine Services

To access our telemedicine services, please follow these steps:

  1. Visit our website and navigate to the Telemedicine section.
  2. Fill out the online registration form.
  3. Schedule an appointment with one of our healthcare providers.
  4. Join the virtual consultation at the scheduled time.

Benefits of Telemedicine

  • Convenience of receiving care from home.
  • Reduced travel time and costs.
  • Access to specialists who may not be available locally.
  • Increased flexibility in scheduling appointments.
